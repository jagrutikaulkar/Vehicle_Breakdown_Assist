Our History

Founded in 2023, VBAS has grown from a local roadside assistance provider to a reputable national service network. Our team of certified professionals is dedicated to ensuring quick response times and high-quality service, utilizing the latest technology to connect drivers with the assistance they need. With a relentless focus on customer satisfaction and safety, we have earned the trust of motorists across the country. Whether it's a flat tire, engine breakdown, or lockout situation, VBAS is committed to delivering prompt and reliable solutions, 24/7, 365 days a year. Our extensive network of service providers ensures that help is never far away, no matter where you are. As we continue to expand our reach and enhance our capabilities, VBAS remains steadfast in our mission to provide peace of mind on the road, one driver at a time.
